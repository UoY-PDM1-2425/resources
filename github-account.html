GitHub Account Setup

All sample code, practical exercises, and their solutions will be stored in GitHub repositories. You will also store all of your work on practical exercises on GitHub. GitHub is a very popular tool used by industry professionals around the world. It is also one of the most common places to host a portfolio to share with potential employers. So, learning how to use it now will help you in your future career.

I do not have a GitHub account

  1. Go to github.com.
  2. Click the "Sign Up" button.
  3. You will be prompted to enter an email address. You can use any email address you like and you can also associate your account with multiple email addresses. If you choose to sign up with a personal email address, we will ask you to link your York email address so that we can easily give you access to course GitHub resources. If you choose to sign up with your York email, we recommend that you also link a personal email address so you don't lose access to your code when you graduate. The instructions for linking additional emails are in the next section.
  4. Follow the rest of the steps in GitHub's account creation process. You will be asked to create a username during sign up. This username will be public so choose something that you're happy for future employers to see!

I already have a GitHub account / I need to link an email address to my GitHub account

If you already have a GitHub account associated with an email address other than your University of York email, you will need to link your uni email so that you can be added to course repos.If you just created a new account with your uni email, it's a good idea to link a personal email address as well. When you have multiple emails associated with your account, you can choose which one receives notifications from GitHub.

  1. Go to github.com and log in.
  2. Click your profile icon in the top right of your GitHub dashboard to open your account menu. If you haven't set your own icon, this will be a circular icon with a pixel-like pattern (everyone has a different pattern).
  3. In the account menu, click "Settings" toward the bottom.
  4. In the left navigation, click "Emails" under the "Access" heading.
  5. Add your additional email address. You can also set your email notifications on this page.
